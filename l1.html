A place pack is where private notes become shared work.

Do not over-claim

An L1 place pack can hold questions and compatibility notes without pretending there is site control, funding, approval or community consent.

L1 questions

What a place mesh needs to know.

Who is the steward?

Name the group or person allowed to review the note before it moves.

What public job does it do?

Notice, repair, training, shelter, event, health logistics, civic rehearsal, transport, food or media.

What inputs are needed?

Maps, photos, opening hours, equipment, transport, power, weather, source links, local observations.

What is the review gate?

Planning, cultural, legal, clinical, safety, insurance, owner, neighbour or venue review.

What public output is safe?

Screen notice, grant evidence, scenario note, source trail, public profile, event packet or aggregated metric.

What can go wrong?

Surveillance creep, stale notices, unsafe instructions, private contact exposure, approval theatre or site speculation.
