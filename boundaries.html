Sovereignty

Your prompt. Your settings. Your sharing path.

This builder starts from self sovereignty. You choose what stays local, what gets shared, who helps tune it, and which version is public.

Owner controls

Set the scene before the generator runs.

Local-only detail

Input names, health context, family context, routines, access paths or raw sensor detail only when you want them in your private version.

Status label

Label concept art, mock-up, grant visual, public page, live site, property idea or source document as different things.

Health visuals

Use health visuals for rooms, flow, comfort and logistics. Bring qualified humans into treatment, diagnosis or device choices.

Tracking choice

Use anonymous groups, flows and placeholders when the point is a public simulation rather than a person tracker.

Cultural care

Use broad context, placeholders and chosen readers for Country, place names, ecology and cultural material.

Freshness date

Add a source date for property, tender, planning, funding and public-source material so the next person knows when to re-check it.
